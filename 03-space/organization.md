# Small-Space Organization

> Apartment storage strategies for baby stuff.

## Principles

1. **Go vertical** — Walls are your friend
2. **Dual-purpose furniture** — Dresser = changing table, ottoman = storage
3. **Rotate, don't hoard** — Only keep current-size items accessible
4. **One in, one out** — Donate as you outgrow

## Storage Ideas

### Closet Optimization
- [ ] Closet dividers by size (NB, 0-3, 3-6, etc.)
- [ ] Hanging organizer for accessories, socks, hats
- [ ] Shelf dividers or bins for folded items
- [ ] Under-hanging-rod space for bins

### Wall-Mounted Solutions
- [ ] Floating shelves for books, decor, supplies
- [ ] Pegboard for frequently-used items
- [ ] Wall-mounted diaper caddy by changing area
- [ ] Over-door organizer for shoes/accessories

### Under-Furniture Storage
- [ ] Flat bins under crib/bed for out-of-season clothes
- [ ] Vacuum bags for storing ahead-size clothes compactly

### Diaper Station
- [ ] Diaper caddy (portable, move room to room)
- [ ] Wall shelf above changing pad for essentials

## What to Store Off-Site or Donate

- Clothes baby has outgrown (donate or store ahead for next kid if planning)
- Gear that's been outgrown (bassinet after transition to crib)
- Gifts you won't use
