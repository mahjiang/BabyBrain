# Baby-Proofing

> You don't need to baby-proof everything now. This becomes relevant as baby becomes mobile (~6 months).

## Timeline

| When | What |
|------|------|
| Before birth | Safe sleep setup, cat boundaries |
| ~4-6 months | Outlet covers, cord management, secure furniture to walls |
| ~6-9 months (crawling) | Gates, cabinet locks, floor sweep for small objects |
| ~9-12 months (pulling up) | Corner guards, toilet locks, stove knob covers |

## Cat-Specific Safety

See [cat prep](cat-prep.md) for full details.
- Keep cat out of sleep space
- Secure cat food/water/litter away from baby's reach (once mobile)

## Apartment Considerations

- [ ] Secure bookshelves and dressers to walls (anti-tip straps)
- [ ] Outlet covers
- [ ] Cord management (blinds, electronics)
- [ ] Cabinet locks for cleaning supplies
- [ ] Gate for kitchen or other areas (if applicable)
- [ ] Window guards / stops (required by law in some areas for upper floors)
- [ ] Check for lead paint (pre-1978 buildings)
