# Nice-to-Have Items

> Items that are helpful but not essential. Good registry additions. Don't buy until you see what you get as gifts and what baby actually likes.

## First 3 Months

| Item | Why | Priority | Notes |
|------|-----|----------|-------|
| Bouncer / rocker | Somewhere safe to set baby down | Medium | Baby Bjorn bouncer is popular; some babies hate them |
| Swing | Soothes some babies | Low | Takes up space; try bouncer first |
| Play mat / activity gym | Tummy time, stimulation | Medium | Useful from ~1 month; Lovevery is popular but pricey |
| Portable sound machine | For outings, naps on the go | Medium | Yogasleep Hushh 2 or similar |
| Diaper bag / backpack | Organized outings | Medium | A regular backpack works fine too |
| Baby bathtub / bath flower | Easier than sink | Low | Skip if sink works for you |
| Night light (dimmable) | Night feeds without waking everyone | Medium | Hatch Rest does double duty as sound machine |

## 3–6 Months

| Item | Why | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Exersaucer / jumper | Contained entertainment once baby can hold head up | Big footprint — skip if space is tight |
| High chair | Starting solids ~6 months | See [high-chair.md](high-chair.md) — Abiie Beyond recommended, IKEA Antilop as backup |
| Teething toys | Some babies teethe early | |
| Board books | Start reading early | Great gift/registry item |

## Nice but Not Necessary

- Baby Brezza (formula machine) — convenient but expensive
- Owlet or similar monitor sock — AAP does not recommend consumer oxygen monitors for healthy infants
- Fancy nursery decor — baby doesn't care
- Baby shoes — not until walking

### Nursing Chair / Glider

*Added 2026-03-01*
*Updated 2026-03-02: Prices verified — significant increases across all models. Nurture& up to $1,199, DaVinci Suzy up to $399–499, Babyletto Toco up to $449–499.*

A nursing chair is **not essential** — your couch, bed, or any comfortable chair with a nursing pillow works. But with 8–12+ feeds per day in the early months, a dedicated chair with good arm support and the ability to rock/glide makes a meaningful comfort difference, especially for night feeds and rocking baby to sleep.

Key note: **All prices have increased significantly since this was last researched.** The Nurture& has gone from ~$750 to ~$1,199. The DaVinci Suzy has gone from ~$300 to $399–499. These are no longer "moderate" purchases. Strong registry candidate for the DaVinci Suzy or Babyletto Toco; fall back to existing furniture + nursing pillow if not gifted.
