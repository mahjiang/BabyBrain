# Baby Clothing Guide

> **Used OK:** YES — clothes are perfect for secondhand, hand-me-downs, and Buy Nothing groups.

## What You Actually Need (Newborn–3 Months)

| Item | Quantity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Zip-up sleepers / footie pajamas | 5-7 | These are the MVP. Day and night outfit. Zips > snaps. |
| Short-sleeve onesies / bodysuits | 5-7 | Layer under sleepers or standalone in warm weather |
| Going-home outfit | 1 | Something cute with easy access (zip or loose) |
| Socks / booties | 3-4 pairs | They fall off constantly. Don't invest heavily. |
| Hat (for sun/warmth) | 1-2 | Newborns lose heat through their heads |
| Scratch mittens | 2 pairs | Or just use sleepers with fold-over cuffs |
| Light jacket / bunting (seasonal) | 1 | If baby arrives in cooler weather |

## Sizing Guide

| Size | Typical Age | Weight |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Preemie | Premature / very small | Up to ~6 lbs |
| Newborn (NB) | 0-4 weeks | Up to ~8 lbs |
| 0-3 months | 0-12 weeks | 8-12 lbs |
| 3-6 months | 3-6 months | 12-16 lbs |

**Tip:** Many full-term babies skip newborn size entirely. Buy more 0-3 month than NB. Due in July = warm weather baby, so lean toward lighter clothes.

## Pro Tips

- Zip-up > snaps (especially at 3 AM)
- Stretchy cotton is easiest to get on/off
- Skip jeans, shoes, and "outfits" for newborns — function over fashion
- Accept all hand-me-downs; donate what you don't use
- Organize by current size + next size in separate bins
