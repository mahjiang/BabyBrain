# Feeding Supplies

> **Status:** Researching | Depends on feeding plan — see also [breastfeeding prep](../06-feeding/breastfeeding-prep.md) and [pumping](../06-feeding/pumping.md)

## What You Need Depends on Your Plan

| Feeding Plan | What to Buy |
|-------------|-------------|
| Exclusively breastfeeding | Nursing pillow, breast pads, nipple cream, nursing bras |
| Pumping + bottles | Pump (insurance), bottles, storage bags, bottle brush |
| Combo feeding | All of the above + formula |
| Formula feeding | Bottles, formula, bottle brush |

**Advice:** Don't overbuy before baby arrives. Start small and see what works. Babies can be picky about bottle shapes/nipples.
