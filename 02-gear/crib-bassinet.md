# Crib / Bassinet

> **Status:** Researching | **Buy by:** Week 28-30 | **Buy new:** YES (safety-critical)

## What to Know

- AAP recommends **room-sharing** (not bed-sharing) for at least 6 months, ideally 12 months (Source: [AAP Safe Sleep](https://publications.aap.org/pediatrics/article/150/1/e2022057990/188304))
- Sleep surface must be **firm, flat, and bare** — no pillows, bumpers, blankets, or toys
- Must meet current CPSC safety standards
- For small spaces, a bassinet or mini crib is often the right first choice

## Our Requirements

- Fits in bedroom next to bed (see [nursery nook](../03-space/nursery-nook.md))
- Cat-safe (needs cover or deterrent — see [cat prep](../03-space/cat-prep.md))
- Small footprint for apartment
- Safe sleep compliant (CPSC, ASTM standards)
