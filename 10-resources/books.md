# Recommended Reading

## Pregnancy & Birth

| Book | Author | Focus | Mini Review |
|------|--------|-------|-------------|
| *Expecting Better* | Emily Oster | Evidence-based pregnancy decisions | Data-driven look at what actually matters. Great for the researcher type. |
| *The Birth Partner* | Penny Simkin | Labor support for partners | The go-to guide for the non-birthing partner. Practical and reassuring. |
| *Ina May's Guide to Childbirth* | Ina May Gaskin | Natural childbirth | Empowering birth stories. More philosophy than data. |

## Newborn & Parenting

| Book | Author | Focus | Mini Review |
|------|--------|-------|-------------|
| *Cribsheet* | Emily Oster | Evidence-based parenting (0-toddler) | Sequel to Expecting Better. Covers breastfeeding, sleep, childcare with data. |
| *Happiest Baby on the Block* | Harvey Karp | Soothing newborns (5 S's) | Practical techniques for calming a fussy baby. |
| *Precious Little Sleep* | Alexis Dubief | Baby sleep | Practical, funny, evidence-informed sleep guide. |
| *Brain Rules for Baby* | John Medina | Child development (0-5) | Neuroscience of early childhood. Accessible and interesting. |
